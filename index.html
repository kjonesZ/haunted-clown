The Traveling Clown That Won’t Leave

A “case file” about a suspicious clown figurine. This is a page that is documenting these cases across southern Ohio.

Disclaimer: By viewing this page, the clown may reappear again at your house and in your closet.
Incident Summary
CASE ID: CLWN-2

The figurine looks harmless—painted clothes, a cane, an awkward smile. But witnesses report the same pattern: it “returns” to the one who purchased it in the hallway, closet, attic, bedroom, treehouse as if waiting.

Pattern reported:b> moved → returned → looking down from top shelves.

What makes it “haunted”

  • Auditory anomaly: cane-like tapping around midnight.
  • Visual anomaly: posture changes between checks.
  • Environmental effect: nearby music glitches or skips.
  • Behavioral effect: pets avoid the room.
